BUSINESS INSIGHTS — SALES ANALYSIS DASHBOARD 2025

1.  PRODUCT REVENUE CONCENTRATION Laptop Pro 14 generated the highest
    product revenue at approximately ₹100.03 million. Laptop Air 13 and
    Smartphone X were also major revenue contributors. This indicates
    that high-value electronics drive a large share of company revenue.

2.  REVENUE AND UNIT SALES ARE DIFFERENT PERFORMANCE METRICS USB-C Hub
    sold the highest number of units at 1,495, but Laptop Pro 14
    generated the highest revenue. Management should not use unit volume
    alone to judge product financial performance because lower-priced
    products can lead in volume without leading in revenue.

3.  REGIONAL PERFORMANCE IS RELATIVELY CLOSE The East region generated
    the highest revenue at ₹76.89 million. However, revenue is
    distributed across all five regions rather than being dominated by
    one region. The company should investigate the sales practices and
    customer mix in the East while continuing to develop
    lower-performing regions.

4.  SALESPERSON PERFORMANCE Arjun Rao generated the highest salesperson
    revenue at ₹54.37 million. The gap between Arjun Rao and the
    remaining salespeople should be investigated to determine whether
    product mix, customer segment, region, or sales practices explain
    the difference.

5.  MONTHLY REVENUE IS VOLATILE Monthly revenue fluctuated
    throughout 2025. May recorded the highest revenue at ₹37.39 million,
    while September recorded the lowest at ₹25.08 million. There is no
    consistent annual growth or decline pattern. Management should
    compare product mix, order volume, discounts, and customer segments
    between high- and low-performing months before making a causal
    conclusion.

FINAL MANAGEMENT SUMMARY

The business generated approximately ₹360.95 million in total revenue
during 2025. High-value electronics are the strongest revenue drivers,
while the highest-volume product is a lower-priced accessory. East is
the leading revenue region and Arjun Rao is the top salesperson. Monthly
revenue is inconsistent, so further analysis should focus on the factors
behind the May peak and September decline.
